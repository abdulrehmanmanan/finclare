THE FOUR QUIET COSTS

These aren't hypothetical. They're the specific, named ways UAE property investors lose money every year without ever seeing it happen.

Cost 01: The Silent Threshold

You crossed AED 375,000 and didn't notice

The moment your income passes this line, corporate tax and VAT rules can activate, depending on how you own the property. Most investors don't track this number at all, which means they don't know the moment it starts to matter.

Cost 02: The Annual Blind Spot

Your accountant only looks once a year

You're paying AED 7,000–9,000/yr for a once-a-year glance backward. Deductions that needed claiming in March don't get flagged until the following March, and by then it's often too late to claim them at all.

Cost 03: The Deduction You Don't Know Exists

Management fees. Service charges. Maintenance.

All legally deductible. Almost none of it gets claimed by foreign investors, because nobody ever explained which receipts matter and which don't.

"I didn't know" is getting more expensive every year

FTA enforcement has tightened steadily since 2023. The penalty for late VAT registration alone is AED 20,000. Waiting doesn't make this go away. It just makes the eventual correction more expensive.
